AT TEPIA

What started as a group of strangers gradually evolved into a team of friendships. Tepia Co. did not only educate their interns about the fundamentals of web development and game programming but taught them how to function in a rigorious work space. Under the mentorship of several professional gentlemen, Ki Song and Ara Agopian, the interns were greeted with ease and a warm comforting presence and environment. In a remotely compact yet comfortable setting, Tepia Co. offers a multitude of Oculus headsets in order to welcome their interns to the world of virtual reality. Along with VR, Tepia Co. provides helpful assistance and steady guidance in order to design and develop a running website. Not only will you have hands on experiences with virtual reality and website development, but the knowledge acquired from the weeks you decide to stay will aid you in your major for years to come. Thus, We highly recommend any student who wishes to indulge in the field of computer science to apply for the internship of this company.
